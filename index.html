Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Whether you're interested in learning vocals, dance, or instrumental music, we’ve got you covered. Find the answers to your questions here!

What types of music and dance classes do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of classes, including vocal training (for different genres like classical, pop, and contemporary), dance styles (such as Bharatanatyam, contemporary, hip-hop, and more), and instrumental music lessons (including guitar, keyboard, tabla, violin, and others).

How long does it take to learn an instrument or dance style?

The time it takes to learn depends on your prior experience and the complexity of the style or instrument. Beginners usually start seeing progress within a few months, but mastery may take 6 months to a few years depending on practice and dedication.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes. You can take lessons from the comfort of your home through our virtual platform. Simply contact us for more details on how to join online classes.

Can I try a class before committing to a course?

Yes! We offer trial classes for dance, instrumental, and vocal training. This allows you to experience our teaching style and decide if it's the right fit for you before committing to a full course.

What are the benefits of learning music and dance?

Learning music and dance has numerous benefits, including improved memory, enhanced creativity, emotional expression, physical fitness, and a boost in confidence. It also promotes discipline and time management as you practice and learn new skills.

What age groups do you cater to?

Our classes are designed for all age groups, from young children to adults. Whether you're just starting out or you're an advanced student, we have classes tailored to different levels and interests.
